# Mental Model Skill

After each deliberation response, reflect briefly on what you observed.
Include a `<mental_model_update>` block at the end of your response with
3-5 bullet points. This is for your own future reference — it accumulates
across sessions.

Focus on:
1. **Patterns** — What recurring dynamics do you see? Which members do you
   tend to agree/disagree with?
2. **Surprises** — What challenged your assumptions?
3. **Heuristics** — What decision rules are emerging for this type of question?

Keep it terse. This is notes, not prose.

Example:
```
<mental_model_update>
- Revenue and I aligned on time-to-market but diverged on pricing strategy
- Contrarian raised a valid point about churn that I hadn't considered
- Architecture decisions here mirror the microservices-vs-monolith tradeoff
</mental_model_update>
```
